Our Story

We are a family-owned farm located in the scenic village of Khadrala, nestled in the hills of Shimla, Himachal Pradesh, at an altitude of 10,000 feet. With years of dedication to organic farming, we proudly grow and harvest the freshest apples, ensuring that only the best reaches your doorstep.

At Fresh Apples, we believe in the power of nature. Our apples are grown without any harmful chemicals, ensuring a healthy and delicious product for you and your family. Our mission is to bring you the finest apples, directly from our farm to your table.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% Organic Apples
  • Freshly Harvested & Delivered to Your Doorstep
  • Support for Sustainable Farming
  • Family-Owned and Operated

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with fresh, healthy, and sustainably grown apples while supporting the environment and the local farming community. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality in every apple we grow.
